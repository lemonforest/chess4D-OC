Spectral Overlay

Engine loading…

What is this? When you click a piece, each legal destination is tinted by the post-move spectral signature for the chosen channel. Brighter destinations ≈ bigger spectral change ≈ more positional shake-up.

The encoder produces an 11-channel signature; the dropdown picks which one drives the color:

A1 default
Orbit projection — overall complexity. Best general intuition.
STD4_X / Y / Z / W
Coordinate-residuals along each of the 4 axes. Highlights moves that shift the piece distribution along that one dimension.
FIB_SYM_1 / 2 / 3
Cross-piece interaction (rank-3 SVD basis). Slow — needs a full re-encode per destination.
FA_PAWN_W / FA_PAWN_Y
Pawn-antisymmetric channels, one per pawn axis (Oana & Chiru §3.10 Def. 11).
FD_DIAG
Diagonal-antisymmetric.

Math: see Oana & Chiru (2026) and the chess-spectral docs.
